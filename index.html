About Me

Not afraid to try anything once - except sky diving, I had to try that three times.

I am currently working with ReactJS providing additional updates to a team project in order to prepare for production deployment. Before that, I completed a personally developed full stack web app called middlewear, an e-commerce website focused on programmer themed t-shirts.

Prior to engaging in my coding interest, I worked with a financial institution and managed their mortgage closings. For about a year I was the only one handling the files for closing until two more people were hired and I passed on my knowledge.

Intrigued by code, I self-taught myself the basics through free resources. I knew I wanted an accelerant to learning and I decided to attend DevMountain, a coding bootcamp. I prepared to dedicate 13 weeks to pure code. Through determination and hardwork I was creating full stack applications when a month prior I didnt even know what a node module was!
